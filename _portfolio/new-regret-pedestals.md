New Regret Pedestals is a fork and extension of the original Regret Pedestals mod for *The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth*. It leaves a fading after-image of items that were not picked up and also remembers blind items that were rerolled with the D6 or a restock machine.

The mod supports Glitched Crown and Tainted Isaac, and is designed to work with the vanilla game without requiring REPENTOGON or Reflourished.

The mod is available on the [Steam Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3672070284), and the source code is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Lei-Tin/Isaac-Regret-Pedestals).
